Welcome to my Game

Welcome to my questions page. This website is about my own project to practice what I have learned so far. The games are based on questions in a selector where you have to choose one of the options to advance until you have the result. I hope you enjoy!!!

Games

You are a Paty or Selma?

From the world of the Simpsons comes this test to determine which of the Bouvier sisters you are a Patty or a Selma. they are related but seeing what your result is you will see who you really are.

Let's Start

Now you will have to answer the following questions by choosing the different options for each one.

Question 1: ¿Qué tipo de películas disfrutas más?
Question 2: ¿Que personaje prefieres?
Question 3: Elige uno de los siguientes numeros
Question 4: ¿Cómo sueles lidiar con el estrés?
